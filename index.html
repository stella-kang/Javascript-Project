    Instructions

    Music:

    • - Feed the Shiba Inu by typing words in the bottom left corner based on the snacks that they're asking for (in any order)
    • - If they ask for more than one of the same snack, you'll need to submit the word again!
    • - The higher the level, the longer their request will be
    • - You'll have 3 chances to feed them the right snacks within the time limit before the game is over

        • Based on your score, you can achieve the following rankings:
      • 1 - 9 points: Junior Snacker
      • 10 - 19 points: Senior Snacker
      • 20 - 29 points: Super Snacker
      • 30+ points: Snack Attacker!
